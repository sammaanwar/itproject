Our Services

The Pottery Hub offers a range of services. We start from basic pottery and go all the way upto intermediate.

  • Beginner:

    Here we start with the basics. No tools, all hands. We create cute little pots and other souvenirs for fun. The price is $30 per class.

  • Medium:

    Here we start to level up and and create larger sculptures. Our art varies from pots to vases and other equipment. The price is $50 per class.

  • Indermediate:

    Once you've mastered the art you are free to join our intermediate classes. These classes are extra complex. We use a vareity of tools and materials. Here we work on more of the larger art pieces. The price per class is $70.
